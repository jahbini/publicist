# LEPA Integration Strategy (Typhoon Slowstep Mode)

## Definition
LEPA = Logos / Ethos / Pathos / Anima

A diagnostic layer that interprets story events as energy misalignments,
used to guide narrative reflection without appearing explicitly in output.

---

## Core Principle

LEPA is NOT part of generation.
LEPA is a PRE-GENERATION diagnostic.

Pipeline pattern:

event_selection
→ lepa_diagnostics
→ narrative_generation

---

## Role in Pipeline

LEPA acts as:

1. Pattern recognizer (what is happening?)
2. Energy classifier (which system is off?)
3. Voice guide (how should this be expressed?)

---

## Minimal Conceptual Flow

1. Events selected
2. Events interpreted through LEPA lens
3. One or two dominant distortions inferred
4. Voice constraints selected
5. Narrative generated using those constraints

---

## LEPA Output (Conceptual Artifact)

lepa_state:
  primary_energy: pathos
  secondary_energy: logos

  primary_distortion: molotovmensch
  secondary_distortion: babbelmensch

  energy_modes:
    pathos: explosive
    logos: chatter

  narrative_guidance:
    voice: jim
    allow_labels: false
    style: observational

---

## Voice Strategy

LEPA must adapt to narrator:

Tommy:
- may use distortion labels directly
- tone: sharp, aggressive

Jim:
- translate into human observation
- tone: grounded, reflective

Southwick:
- describe mechanism precisely
- tone: clinical, minimal

Roger:
- imply, do not state
- tone: drifting, incomplete

---

## Distortion Role

Distortions are NOT content.
They are INTERNAL SIGNALS.

They influence:
- reflection phrasing
- realization tone
- narrative emphasis

They should rarely appear explicitly.

---

## Design Constraint

Never allow LEPA to:
- dominate the story
- introduce abstract language
- override event sequence

It only shapes HOW the story is told.

---

## Strategic Insight

LEPA converts:

raw events → human meaning

It is the bridge between:
- structure (pipeline)
- voice (narrative)
- insight (reader impact)

---

## Slowstep Guideline

Keep LEPA simple at first:

- choose ONE primary distortion
- optionally ONE secondary
- guide ONLY the reflection section

Do not attempt full-system control early.

---

## One-Line Summary

LEPA is the quiet layer that turns events into insight without announcing itself.
